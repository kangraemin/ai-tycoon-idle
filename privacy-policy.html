Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

AI Tycoon ("the Game") is a free idle game developed by an individual developer. This page explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

The Game collects the following data:

  • Game save data — stored locally on your device (localStorage / device storage). This data never leaves your device.
  • Anonymous usage statistics — via Google Analytics 4. This includes session duration, screen views, and in-game events (e.g. upgrades purchased, challenges completed). No personally identifiable information is collected.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics 4 — used to understand how players interact with the game. Data is collected anonymously. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob — the Game may display ads in future updates. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. See Google Ads Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties beyond the services listed above.

Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
